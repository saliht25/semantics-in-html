Some of the benefits from writing semantic markup are as follows:
  • Search engines will consider its contents as important keywords to influence the page's search rankings (see SEO)
  • Screen readers can use it as a signpost to help visually impaired users navigate a page
  • Finding blocks of meaningfull code is significantly easier than searching through endless divs with or without semantic or name spaced classes
  • Suggests to the developer the type of data that will be populated
  • Semantic naming mirrors proper custom element/component naming

SEO

⭐SEO (Search Engine Optamization) is the process of making a website more visible in search results, also termed improving search rankings

⭐Search engines crawl teh web, following links from page to page, and index the content found. When you search, the search engine displays from the indexed content. Crawlers follow rules. If you follow those rules closely when doing SEO for website, you give the site the best chances of showing up among the first results, increasing traffic and possibly revenue (for ecommerce and ads ).

⭐Search engines give some guidelines for SEO, but bif search engines keep result ranking as a trade secret. SEO combines official search engine guidelines, empirical knowledge, and theoritical knowledgefrom science papers or paterts.
